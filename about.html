About

Hi, my name is Sean Vinci. I’m a designer, developer, and musician from New York.

I’m currently working as a designer and front-end developer for The School of The New York Times. I've also worked with companies like Weight Watchers and The Museum of Modern Art.

I’m currently drumming with alt. rock group, Paracutá and singer/songwriter/composer, Oliver Thompson. I’m available for live and studio work.

From time to time I also work as a producer and recording/mixing engineer. I have been fortunate to work with amazing people like Stanley Brown, Rodney Jerkins, LL Cool J, Melanie Fiona, Kirk Franklin, Tim Huges, Jill Scott, Hezekiah Walker, Le’Andria Johnson, Cory Henry; and companies like BET Network, Universal Music Group, Sony Music Entertainment, RCA Records, Verizon, University of Michigan, General Motors, MTV.
